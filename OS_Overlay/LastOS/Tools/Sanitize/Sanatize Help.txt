Usage:

Edit C:\Windows\Removes.ini to add word to be removed, by putting a * before a word means it will be removed from the middle of another word.

Holding CTRL when you execute will remove all text before the episode number of a series (useful if you wish to remove the name of a series from the file name).
* If you also hold Shift while holding Ctrl then it will only keep the Series name and Episode Number instead.
* Holding CTRL while on Movie (no Episode Numbers) will crop it at 1080p or 720p (Shift has no effect)

Holding Pause when you execute will remove all the " - " seperator from your file names.

Holding Scroll Lock when you execute will place brackets around any year date "2016" = "(2016)"

* You can combine holding keys when you execute
